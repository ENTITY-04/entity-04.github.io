About Cats

Tiny tigers with giant personalities.

Every kitty carries a sprinkle of mystery. Here are a few delightful facts to kickstart your curiosity.

  • 🐱 Cats have a specialized collarbone that lets them always land on their feet—called the righting reflex.
  • 🌀 A cat's whiskers are as wide as their body, helping them judge tight spaces in complete darkness.
  • 💤 The average cat naps 12–16 hours a day; deep sleep fuels their crepuscular playtimes.
  • 🎵 Cats make over 100 vocal sounds, from gentle chirps to authoritative yowls.

Cat Care

Simple routines for happy whiskers.

🥣

Balanced Meals

Mix wet and dry food with fresh water daily. Adjust portions for age, activity, and vet guidance.

🪶

Play & Enrichment

Short play bursts twice a day keep instincts sharp—rotate wand toys, puzzle feeders, and cardboard forts.

🧼

Groom & Glow

Weekly brushing reduces shedding and hairballs. Pair grooming with treats to build trust.

🩺

Vet Check-ins

Schedule annual wellness visits and keep vaccines current. Track any changes in appetite or energy.
