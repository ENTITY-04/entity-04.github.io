About Cats

Cat Facts: Tiny Tigers with Giant Personalities.

Every kitty carries a sprinkle of mystery. Here are a few delightful cat facts to kickstart your curiosity.

  • 🐱 Cats have a specialized collarbone that lets them always land on their feet—called the righting reflex.
  • 🌀 A cat's whiskers are as wide as their body, helping them judge tight spaces in complete darkness.
  • 💤 The average cat naps 12–16 hours a day; deep sleep fuels their crepuscular playtimes.
  • 🎵 Cats make over 100 vocal sounds, from gentle chirps to authoritative yowls.

Cat Care

Cat Care: Simple Routines for Happy Whiskers.

🥣

Cat Nutrition & Balanced Meals

Mix wet and dry food with fresh water daily. Adjust portions for age, activity, and vet guidance.

🪶

Cat Toys & Enrichment

Short play bursts twice a day keep instincts sharp—rotate cat wand toys, puzzle feeders, and cardboard forts.

🧼

Cat Grooming Tips

Weekly cat grooming reduces shedding and hairballs. Pair brushing with treats to build trust.

🩺

Cat Health Check-ins

Schedule annual cat health wellness visits and keep vaccines current. Track any changes in appetite or energy.

🪷

Cat Dental Care

Brush your cat's teeth a few times a week with cat-safe toothpaste. Dental treats and water additives help keep plaque at bay.

🧹

Litter Box Hygiene

Scoop daily and do a full litter change weekly. Keep one more litter box than the number of cats, placed in quiet spots.

🛋️

Cat Rest & Cosy Spaces

Provide warm, elevated resting spots away from drafts. Cats sleep 12–16 hours a day—quality rest supports their cat health.

🏠

Safe Indoor Environment

Cat-proof your home by securing toxic plants, loose cables, and small objects. Window screens let cats enjoy fresh air safely.

💧

Cat Hydration

Many cats prefer running water—a cat water fountain encourages drinking and supports kidney and urinary cat health.

⚖️

Cat Weight Management

Overweight cats face serious cat health risks. Use portion-controlled meals, feeding puzzles, and active cat toys to maintain a healthy weight.
